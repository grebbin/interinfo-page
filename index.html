What is Parallax Scrolling?

Parallax scrolling is a design technique that creates a sense of movement, fluidity and depth. It is a way of adding a more or less faux three-dimensionality and therefore bringing immersion and life to the web experience. The effect can be used to display content dynamically in an eye grabbing way and is easy to understand for visitors, because it borrows from what we see in our everyday life.11

Uniqueness of Parallax Scrolling

No need to decipher what can be clicked and what to type into a text field. Parallax scrolling doesn’t demand complicated input and understanding of the web. It happens in addition and bursts to life while traversing the web page through scrolling.11 The effect is not a good fit for every side and can be distracting, overbearing and confusing (like this paragraph). But if done right, it has the potential to keep the visitor engaged and encourage them to continue the scrolling.44
